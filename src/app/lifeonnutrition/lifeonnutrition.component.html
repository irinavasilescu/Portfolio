LIFE ON NUTRITION

LIFE ON NUTRITION is a web application developed using Yii, MySQL, Angular, Angular Material, RxJS, CSS, HTML, Amazon Web Services (EC2). It was built for a small business in the nutritional market. The application stores data such as minimal client information, ingredients and their nutritional values, recipes, menus and shopping lists. The export of shopping lists and menus makes it easy to send them to clients via email. It allows fast and easy creation of weekly menus for clients by offering the posibility of importing existent recipes. Also, it automatically calculates the desired nutritional values for the specific stages in the clients' diets. It is currently under development, new functionalities are being added as you read.
